Dia's recipe

Ingredients

For the devil's balls

  • Half a cup of powdered sugar
  • One quarter teaspoon salt
  • One knifetip Turkish hash
  • Half a pound butter
  • One teaspoon vanilla-sugar
  • Half a pound flour
  • 150 g ground nuts
  • A little extra powdered sugar
  • ... and no eggs

Instructions

  1. Place in a bowl.
  2. Stir in butter.
  3. Add the ground nuts.
  4. and knead the dough.
  5. Form eyeball-size pieces from the dough.
  6. Roll in the powdered sugar and say the Magic Words: "Simsalabimbamba Saladu Saladim"
  7. Place on a greased baking pan and Bake at 200 degrees for 15 minutes ...AND NO EGGS
  8. Bake at 200 degrees for 15 minutes
